My favorite hobby is scrapbooking. I learned over 25 years ago and have continued to enjoy this relaxing art. When I need to unwind, I go to my Cricut Explore machines that I absolutely love and use different kinds of materials to make scrapbooking creations. I use my Cricut Explore 3 machine and my Sizzix Big Shot machine (the embosser). I have so many stamps and paper with beautiful colors, designs, and textures.

I enjoy making cards for my friends and family, and making banners for a birthday celebration or retirement party. While I do enjoy doing these projects, they are time-consuming. Still, the end product is well worth the time invested. I have a scrapbook for each of my children, then each year to capture the crucial moments in our lives, I must have over 16 photo scrapbooks. During each trip to Disneyland "The Happiest place on earth" we take around 300 photos and turn them into a Disney-themed scrapbook. Other albums have different themes such as floral, Marvel or Star Wars.

Working on arts and crafts is relaxing for me, I could have music playing or be completly in silence in the craft room. Either way I enjoy my time cutting, putting together each project's big and tiny pieces. I greatly enjoy using the Cricut Explore machine. It takes a bit to select which character(s) I will be using, sizing it, and then watching it cut layer by layer. The challenging part is putting it together, some parts are so tiny and intricate, but the end project is worth the time and effort. I also enjoy working on DC Comics and Harry Potter themed cards. My office/craft room has so much of this, and scrapbooking was a big part of making the banners and borders for the litographs that decorate the office.

My niece and nephews know Tia Mely has a big selection of craft supplies for their school projects, they check the scrapbook Dreambox (scrapbook armoire) first before they start their poster and presentations. The Dreambox is a piece of furniture that opens up to 9 feet wide. It has a built-in table that unfolds, which can be used as a workbench for crafts and projects. It also has a built-in light at the top of the armoire. It has enough space in the various drawers, shelves, and cupboards to store all my machines and my craft supplies, including paints, colored pens/pencils, adhesives, trimmers, embelishments, glitter, cutting boards, thumbtacks, buttons, etc. I can store anything I need to create a card, page, bannet or even a poster. When closed, it looks like an armoire cabinet.

I am sharing my favorite Youtube videos, which have instructions that show you how to get started; step by step on the Cricut Machine, Design space to create your art, or simple cards. Your creative instinct will kick in once your make a few cards, banners and/or pages.
